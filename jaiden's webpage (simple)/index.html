Jaiden Malia

Based in the Bay Area and NYC

JAIDEN MALIA IS A FILIPINO AMERICAN CREATIVE FROM THE BAY AREA.

SHE EARNED HER BA IN VISUAL ARTS MEDIA FROM THE UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, SAN DIEGO AND IS CURRENTLY ATTENDING NEW YORK UNIVERSITY FOR HER GRADUATE STUDIES.

HER PERSONAL WORK EXPLORES A BROAD RANGE OF TOPICS FROM THE GEN Z POP CULTURE TO SATIRICAL HORRORS OF FEMININITY, OFTEN AIMING

TO REJECT AND RESHAPE THE ESTABLISHED TRADITIONAL NARRATIVES REVOLVING RACEN AND GENDER.
